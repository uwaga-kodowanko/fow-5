Future of Work

Future of Work #5

Hackerspace Trójmiasto and Toptal invite you to join the Future of Work online event - a conversation about changs in the work environment, professional development, and best practices in the workplace.

We are a community of brilliant professionals: developers, designers, remote workers, freelancers, coaches, founders, entrepreneurs, and everyone in between. If you are curious about work transformation and professional growth, these talks are for you!

For the 5th edition of Future of Work, we have asked the speakers to share their thoughts on making the best of and adapting to the current employment conditions. While they might not be the most favorable ones, it doesn’t mean that you can’t thrive in your job. Join us on June 17th, 2020 to learn about it firsthand.

This is an open conversation, so there will also be an opportunity to ask questions and talk to speakers and the Hackerspace Trojmiasto community.

Speakers

Anne-Laure Le Cunff

Founder, Ness Labs

Founder of Ness Labs, a platform providing content, community, and coaching to professionals who don't want sacrificing their mental health, she is passionate about working together and community participation.

In her session “WIP: Working in Public”, Anne-Laure will share her journey from a startup founder to indie maker and how it aligns with working in the open - a practice of sharing progress, asking for feedback, and building products when lots of people are watching.

Sign up for the Future of Work online event to learn about her transition and see how it can help you navigate your own career journey.

Twitter Anne-Laure Le Cunff Linkedin Anne-Laure Le Cunff

Olha Bahaieva

Senior UX/UI designer, Toptal

Olha Bahaieva, Senior UX/UI designer at Toptal and six-time winner in international design contests, will share with you her professional secrets and tell you how to launch your career without wasting time, what aspects of work to focus on and put the user in the center of the design.

Her professional experience speaks for itself - she has created 50+ eCommerce, 20 B2B, and 10 B2C designs as well as directed 5+ startup products.

Don’t miss Olha sharing her practical tips on the 17th of June and prepare your questions - she will answer them during the Q&A session.

Twitter Olha Bahaieva Linkedin Olha Bahaieva

Heidi Araya

Agile Coach & Transformation Leader

Heidi brings more than 20 years of experience in process improvement, project & program management, as well as Agile team coaching and leadership. She worked with a great number of well-known organizations and even NASA Kennedy Space where she led the first Scrum teams.

Join us next Wednesday to learn from her about digital transformation with distributed teams - what opportunities and challenges present themselves and how to make it work in the everchanging professional environment.

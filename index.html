Future of Work

Future of Work #5

Hackerspace Trójmiasto and Toptal invite you to join the Future of Work online event - a conversation about changs in the work environment, professional development, and best practices in the workplace.

We are a community of brilliant professionals: developers, designers, remote workers, freelancers, coaches, founders, entrepreneurs, and everyone in between. If you are curious about work transformation and professional growth, these talks are for you!

For the 5th edition of Future of Work, we have asked the speakers to share their thoughts on making the best of and adapting to the current employment conditions. While they might not be the most favorable ones, it doesn’t mean that you can’t thrive in your job. Join us on June 17th, 2020 to learn about it firsthand.

This is an open conversation, so there will also be an opportunity to ask questions and talk to speakers and the Hackerspace Trojmiasto community.

Speakers

Anne-Laure Le Cunff

Founder, Ness Labs

Anne-Laure Le Cunff is the founder of Ness Labs, a platform providing content, community, and coaching to knowledge workers and creators who want to achieve more without sacrificing their mental health. She studies neuroscience at King's College London. Her work has been featured in WIRED, Forbes, Rolling Stone, and more.

Twitter Anne-Laure Le Cunff Linkedin Anne-Laure Le Cunff

Olha Bahaieva

Senior UX/UI designer, Toptal

A six-time winner in international design contests, Olha comes to Toptal with more than seven years of experience in web and UI/UX design. She has created 50+ eCommerce, 20 B2B, and 10 B2C designs as well as directed 5+ startup products, specializing in UX/UI, branding, and ideation. Olha always puts the user at the center of the design and strives to create innovative units focused on interactivity.

Twitter Olha Bahaieva Linkedin Olha Bahaieva

Heidi Araya

Agile Coach & Transformation Leader

Heidi Araya (MBA, PMP, SAFe SPC, CAL, CSP-SM, CSP-PO) teaches, mentors, and coaches leaders, managers, and teams to improve delivery and business results through Lean-Agile ways of working. Heidi’s expertise includes Lean-Agile Portfolio Management, Agile Transformations, Agile HR, and Agile team coaching.

Future of Work

Future of Work #5

The Future of Work returns for the 5th time, now in the online format. Join us on Wednesday 17 june 2020 for a conversation about changes in the professional environment.

How do we keep up with the ever-changing work environment? The new work realities have affected everyone - freelancers, remote workers, contractors, and everyone in between. Once a month we invite different professionals to share their thoughts on the future of work and gather to discuss their insights.

As always, there will be Q&A after each talk and you will have an opportunity to ask questions and connect with speakers.
